Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish best served fresh out of the oven and hot, unlike revenge, which as we all know should be served with a side salad and full selection of cutlery.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • White sauce
  • Minced meat

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180c
  2. Cook the minced meat
  3. Add the tomato sauce and simmer
  4. Alternate layers in a square ovenproof dish of minced meat, pasta sheet and white sauce.
  5. Bake in oven for 25 minutes
  6. Remove and serve with cold revenge
